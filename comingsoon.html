Technologies:

MONGODB | EXPRESS | NODEJS

This app was inspired by the fact that I have been in some fundraising situations. Most of the time, you just want to enter the name, amount received and let some application do the rest. Rest being calculating the totals and setting some visual representation of how far you are with the target.

I plan to use the technologies stated above as they are the ones I'd like to specialise on. Plus, I worked on a previous project using the same, so it would be a good practice to have another app with a similar stack.
